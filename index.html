Who We Are

We are an engineering consultancy and product design company focused on helping businesses, institutions, and organizations leverage technology to streamline their operations. We also collaborate with companies who desire to develop new products for the mass market.

Vision

REST (Robotics, Engineering, Science & Technology) Solutions is a company whose vision is to advance the learning, familiarization and use of technology in Africa.

Mission

Using Education, and Product & Industrial Design in Robotics, IoT and Automation to meet the growing technological needs of Africa, as we make the transition towards the Fourth Industrial Revolution.
